Simple Listing Policy

Short and clear rules for adding property listings on Eagles Hub.

1. What this is

This page tells you the rules for posting a property. It is short. It uses simple words so everyone can read it.

2. Who can list

  • Landlords who own the property.
  • Agents who have permission from the landlord.
  • People must be 18 years or older.

3. What you can list

  • Houses, apartments, rooms for rent.
  • Short-term or long-term rentals.
  • Clear photos and correct price.

4. What you must not post

  • No fake listings. Do not copy or steal other people’s ads.
  • No illegal properties or things that break the law.
  • No hateful, violent, or harmful content.

5. Tell the truth

  • Write the correct price and correct address.
  • Say if bills or service fees are included or not.
  • Show clear photos. Do not use trick images.

6. Safety and law

Your listing must follow the country laws and local rules. Eagles Hub also follows government policies while we build the site. If a law or rule says something different, we will follow the law.

If you are not sure about a law, check with a local office or lawyer first.

7. Fees and payment

We will say clearly if there is a fee for listing or promoting. Do not send money outside the site unless we tell you. Keep payment records and receipts.

8. When things go wrong (errors & broken pages)

We are still building parts of the site. You may see an error or a page that does not work. This can happen sometimes.

If you see errors, please tell us. Tell us what you did and what you saw. This helps us fix it fast.
How to report:
Report on our Contact Page Email: contact@engleshib.web.app

9. Changes to this policy

We may change these rules as we grow. When we change, we will post the new policy here. Check this page now and then.

10. Questions or help

If you need help, visit our contact page or send an email. We will try to reply quickly.
